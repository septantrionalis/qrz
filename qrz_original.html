I started learning morse code in the middle of 2022. I feel comfortable rag chewing at around 20 WPM. I can do upwards of 25 WPM for quick exchanges if your send is good (e.g. POTA or contest exchanges). As of early 2023, I operate strictly on CW.

SKCC #: 26627T
OMISS #: 7415

My base station is an ICOM 7300, Hustler 6 band vertical antenna, and an Ameritron AL-811 amplifier. I also have an ICOM 208H for the truck, a Yaesu FTM-10R for the motorcycle, and a Yaesu VX-8R handheld transceiver when mobile on my feet.

My equipment of choice for POTA activations is an ICOM 705 using 10 watts with the CHA MPAS 2.0 in the vertical configuration.

For those trying to remember my callsign, think "Zombie Pepper Spray" (KC0ZPS).  By default, I don't send QSL cards for POTA contacts (hunting or activating), but if we do make a POTA exchange and you want one of my zombie pepper spray cards, reach out to me and I'll be more than happy to send you one!
